What is Developing Diversity in STEM?

  • To be source of support and resource for people of marginalized identities.
  • To help prepare those students for the unique challenges we face in this area of study, and specific fields within it.
  • To give students the opportunity to build relationships with fellow STEM students

What makes Developing Diversity different?

Developing Diversity fills a gap that other organizations can't fill.

Other Diversity Orgs

  • A focus on supporting STEM specific needs, without taking away from their focus.

Other Stem Orgs

  • Support students face challenges that we uniquely face, such as identifying and overcoming wash-out programs

We can collaborate with these organizations to fill this gap and help as many students as possible.

Why become an official student organization?

    With the resources JCCC can provide us, we can do so much more

  • Getting the word farther than we could alone
  • A place to hold meetings and events
  • Outreach programs
  • Student-led workshops and seminars
  • Collaboration with faculty
  • Hosting diverse professionals in the STEM field to:
    • Participate in panels for all of JCCC to benefit from.
    • Give advice.
    • Provide representation that is sorely lacking within STEM
