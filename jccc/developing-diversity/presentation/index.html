What is Developing Diversity in STEM?

  • To be source of support and resource for people of marginalized identities.
  • To help prepare those students for the unique challenges we face in this area of study, and the specific fields within it.
  • To give STEM students the opportunity build relationships with fellow students whos stories rhyme with their own.

What makes Developing Diversity different?

Developing Diversity fills a gap that other organizations don't.

Other Diversity Organizations

  • We will have a focus on supporting their STEM specific needs, without taking away from the other organization's larger scopes.

Other Stem Organizations

  • We will focus on helping students prepare to face challenges that we uniquely face in this area of study. Challenges such as identifying and overcoming wash-out programs, discrimination in software development, and much more.

If made official, we can collaborate with these organizations to fill this gap and help as many students as possible.

Why become an official student organization?

With the resources JCCC can provide us, we can do so much more than we could on our own.

  • Getting the word out farther.
  • A place to hold meetings and events.
  • Outreach programs.
  • Student-led workshops and seminars.
  • Collaboration with faculty.

Bringing Diversity here.

  • One major goal is to host diverse professionals in the STEM field to:
    • Participate in panels for all of JCCC to benefit from.
    • Be a part of networking events focusing on diversity.
    • Give advice.
    • Provide a source of representation that is sorely lacking within STEM.
